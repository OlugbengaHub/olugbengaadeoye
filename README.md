### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### 📊 Online Sales Analysis Dashboard
> **Power BI · DAX · SQL Server · Star Schema**

An end-to-end interactive sales intelligence dashboard covering FY2009–2011 global e-commerce data, built to answer six core business questions across sales, product, customer, and executive audiences.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Sales (excl. unknown) | **$16.25M** |
| YoY Sales Growth | **88.7%** |
| Repeat Customer Rate | **98.1%** (5,767 / 5,878 customers) |
| Average Order Value | **$447.01** |
| Top Product | **REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER — $286.49K** |
| Top 10 Products Revenue Share | **~14.7%** |

**What was built:**
- ⭐ Star schema data model — `FactSales` + `DimDate`, `DimProduct`, `DimCustomer`
- 📐 20+ DAX measures including YoY%, contextual `RANKX`, dynamic text narratives, and % of total
- 🗺️ Azure Maps page encoding country revenue and YoY growth simultaneously
- 👥 Customer retention analysis: repeat rate, MoM growth, and cohort tracking
- 🔍 Data quality segmentation: explicit "including / excluding unknown" measures
- 📈 Custom KPIs: Product Repeatability Index and Product Diversity Index

**Key business questions answered:**
1. How are total sales trending over time, and what is the YoY growth rate?
2. Which are the top 10 products and customers by revenue, and what % of total do they represent?
3. Which countries drive the most revenue, and how are they growing YoY?
4. What is the repeat customer rate, and how is the customer base growing month over month?
5. What is the average order value (AOV) and average price per unit?
6. Which single product is the top performer by sales?
